About me

I help people better understand themselves, take a fresh look at how they build their lives, solve existing problems and understand the reasons for their occurrence. In my work, I consider it important not only to solve specific problems and get rid of symptoms, but also to improve the quality of life in general and self-understanding. This makes a person more resistant to life's difficulties and is a prevention of the recurrence of crises.

In simple words, the resource state is the presence of physical and mental strength in a person to solve the upcoming tasks.

Resource state - a certain reserve of energy that allows you to do everything quickly and efficiently, while remaining in a good mood.

In recent years, more and more people are beginning to take care of their psychological health and listen to themselves. Meditations, various trainings and psychological practices are becoming popular.
